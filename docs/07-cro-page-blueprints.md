# CRO Page Blueprints

## Conversion strategy

Sell premium by making the visitor feel:

1. This is a real brand.
2. The product is curated for women like her.
3. The order is low-risk because COD and confirmation are clear.
4. The price is justified by presentation, ritual, trust, and proof.
5. The best value is the bundle, then the cart cross-sell, then the one-time upsell.

## Home page blueprint

1. Announcement bar: COD + UAE + limited offer.
2. Hero with premium image and bundle CTA.
3. Trust row immediately under CTA.
4. Social proof strip.
5. Product cards.
6. Emotion section: tired of random products/no premium feeling.
7. Authority section: curation, usage, QC, packaging.
8. Routine section: how to use.
9. Reviews.
10. Comparison table.
11. FAQ.
12. Final CTA.

## Product page blueprint

### Above the fold

Must include:

- Product name.
- Emotional headline.
- Stars/review count.
- Price.
- Offer selector.
- Scarcity line.
- CTA.
- COD reassurance.

Bundle example:

```text
باقة ليالي بيوتي الفاخرة
رائحة ناعمة، إحساس مرتب، وتفاصيل فاخرة تليق بك.
★★★★★ أكثر اختياراً هذا الأسبوع
AED 299
الدفع عند الاستلام داخل الإمارات
```

### Section order

1. Hero.
2. Sticky social proof: reviews + COD + delivery.
3. Problem agitation:
   - products look cheap
   - random scents do not feel elegant
   - uncertainty from dropshipping-style stores
4. Brand solution.
5. Ingredient/notes story.
6. Ritual/usage steps.
7. Real-life scenarios.
8. Reviews.
9. Risk reversal.
10. FAQ.
11. Final offer.

## Cart drawer CRO

The cart drawer should not feel like a technical cart. It should feel like a confirmation step.

Elements:

- `طلبك محفوظ الآن`
- Item summary.
- Cross-sell title: `أضيفي لمسة تكمل طلبك`
- Cross-sell rationale:
  - `الأكثر إضافة مع الباقة`
  - `بدل AED 128، خذيهما بـ AED 99`
- COD reassurance.
- CTA: `إتمام الطلب`

## Checkout popup CRO

Elements:

- Title: `أكملي الطلب بالدفع عند الاستلام`
- Summary.
- Two fields only.
- Trust microcopy:
  - `لن يتم طلب أي دفع الآن.`
  - `سنتواصل معك لتأكيد الطلب قبل الشحن.`
- Scarcity:
  - `العرض الحالي محجوز لك أثناء تعبئة البيانات.`
- Social proof:
  - `نساء كثيرات يفضلن الدفع عند الاستلام لتجربة أوضح.`

## Post-submit upsell CRO

Goal: increase AOV without hurting lead/order capture.

Rules:

- Show only after order is saved.
- Price AED 39.
- Timer 10-15s.
- Make decline easy.
- Do not block order success if timer expires.

Copy:

```text
عرض خاص مع طلبك فقط
أضيفي سيروم إضافي بـ AED 39 فقط بدل السعر الكامل.
يظهر هذا العرض مرة واحدة بعد تأكيد الطلب.
```

Buttons:

- `أضيفيه لطلبي بـ 39 درهم`
- `لا شكراً، أكملي طلبي`

## Thank-you page CRO

Purpose: reduce cancellation and increase delivery confirmation.

Copy:

```text
تم استلام طلبك بنجاح
طلبك بالدفع عند الاستلام، وسيتواصل معك فريق ليالي بيوتي لتأكيد التفاصيل قبل الشحن.
```

Show:

- Order ID.
- Customer phone masked.
- Item summary.
- Total.
- What happens next:
  1. تأكيد الطلب.
  2. تجهيز الطلب.
  3. التوصيل والدفع عند الاستلام.

## Authority and proof checklist

Use these site-wide:

- Real brand name/logo.
- UAE delivery/COD details.
- Support/contact details.
- Reviews clearly marked as real once collected.
- Ingredient/note education.
- Usage instructions.
- Packaging/QC promise.
- Return/replacement policy.
- Privacy note for phone use.

## Confirmation/delivery improvements

- Thank-you page tells buyer a confirmation contact is coming.
- Backend can later send WhatsApp/SMS confirmation.
- Sheet includes status columns for confirmation team.
- Use clear COD language everywhere so buyers remember they ordered.
- Capture UTM/ad source to measure low-quality traffic causing cancellations.
