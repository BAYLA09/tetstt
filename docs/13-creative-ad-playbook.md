# Creative Ad Playbook — Layali Beauty (2026)

> Internal creative brief for the AI-generated paid social workflow.
> Audience: UAE women, 23–45, Arabic-first, Khaleeji-leaning. Channels: TikTok + Snapchat + Meta Reels. Funnel: cold → COD product page (`/products/luxury-bundle`, `/products/white-rain-musk-serum`, `/products/dubai-palace-oud-serum`).
> Tools assumed available: Sora 2 / Veo 3 / Kling 2 / Runway Gen-4 for video, Nano Banana Pro for avatar stills, ElevenLabs (Arabic v3) + HeyGen / Hedra for lipsync, CapCut for assembly.

## 0. Brand DNA — non-negotiables

Pulled from `/docs/01-product-and-offer-brief.md` and `/docs/06-brand-positioning-and-arabic-copy.md` and the live site copy (home + product pages):

- Positioning: **فخامة هادئة تليق بيومك** — soft luxury, feminine, Khaleeji.
- Promise: COD only, تأكيد قبل الشحن, premium feel, no online payment friction.
- Hero offer: `LB-BUNDLE-299` — باقة ليالي بيوتي الفاخرة (AED 299).
- Add-ons: `LB-SERUM-MUSK-59` مسك المطر الأبيض (AED 59), `LB-SERUM-OUD-69` عود قصر دبي (AED 69).
- Tone: warm, feminine, premium, direct. Khaleeji-friendly Arabic.
- Forbidden language (will get flagged + breaks brand): يعالج، يبيض، يزيل نهائياً، مضمون 100%، معتمد طبياً، خالي من الحساسية، fake certifications, fake exact stock counters.
- Allowed proof: ritual, packaging, COD safety, تأكيد قبل الشحن, scent storytelling, identity/feeling.

Every creative below respects this. We sell HARD on **emotion, identity, scent fantasy, social proof feel** — not medical or transformation claims.

## 1. The 2026 cold-traffic stop-scroll framework

What is actually winning on UAE TikTok / Snap on women's beauty in 2026:

### 1.1 The 0–3 second hook stack (pick 1 per video)

| # | Hook archetype | Why it works on UAE feminine cold traffic | Example (Arabic) |
|---|---|---|---|
| H1 | **POV reveal** — first-person hands, no face | Feels native, looks like a friend filming | `POV: زوجي ما عرف من أين الريحة` |
| H2 | **Curiosity gap statement** | Interrupts scroll because brain must close the loop | `كل واحدة في دبي تسأل عن هذي الريحة وما حد يقول` |
| H3 | **Authority drop** | Trusted-figure avatar with confident first frame | `أنا خبيرة عطور وراح أقولكم سر بنات الإمارات` |
| H4 | **Tactile macro** | Extreme close-up texture / drop falling — pure ASMR | (no text) golden serum drop in slow-mo |
| H5 | **Pattern-break number** | Specific number breaks the scroll | `٣ قطرات بس وكل المجلس يسأل` |
| H6 | **Social-proof stitch fake** | Looks like reaction to a "viral" comment | `شفتوا التعليق هذا؟ خلاص لازم أقول الحقيقة` |
| H7 | **Identity callout** | Names the segment | `لو إنتي بنت إماراتية تحبين الفخامة الهادية، اسمعي` |
| H8 | **Forbidden-secret framing** | "I shouldn't be saying this" | `ما تخليه يطلع بس أنا راح أقولكم` |

Rule: **first frame = motion + face or hands or product close-up + Arabic caption already on screen**. No fade-ins. No logos. No "subscribe" energy. The first 0.7s decides everything.

### 1.2 Pacing rules (2026 algorithm-tuned)

- Cut every 0.6–1.2s for the first 6 seconds, then 1.5–2s after.
- Total length: **15s for cold**, 22s for warmer retargeting, 30s for VSL-style authority.
- Caption track ALWAYS on (Arabic, word-by-word highlight, big bold cream-gold).
- VO is sound-on layer. Captions carry sound-off viewers.
- End with a **loop-back frame** (last 0.5s = same composition as first frame) so the algorithm replays.
- Always 1 CTA at second 7, 1 mid-CTA verbal at 50%, 1 closer CTA card.

### 1.3 The ad-set structure (run this matrix, not single videos)

For each product: **5 concepts × 3 hook variants × 2 VO variants × 2 thumbnail-frame variants = 60 testable assets per product**. Burn fast on TikTok Spark / Snap Story Ads. Kill anything below 1.0 thumb-stop ratio @ 24h.
