# Brand Positioning and Arabic Copy

Layali Beauty is a premium Arabic beauty ritual brand for UAE women who want soft luxury, feminine presence, and confidence in daily self-care.

Core idea: `فخامة هادئة تليق بيومك.`

Voice: Arabic first, warm, feminine, premium, direct. Avoid fake medical, certification, or guaranteed-result language.

Use proof language such as `مختارة بعناية لتناسب الذوق الخليجي.` and `الدفع عند الاستلام لتطلبي بثقة.` Do not show fake certification badges.
