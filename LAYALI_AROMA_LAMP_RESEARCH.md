# Layali Aroma Lamp Product Research

## الخلاصة

إذا بغينا ندخلو فمنتج aroma lamp/gadget، ما نختاروش flame diffuser العادي كـ hero product.

السبب: flame diffuser متباع بزاف ورخيص فـ UAE. كاين بزاف فـ Noon/Amazon بأسعار تقريبية AED 48-160، وحتى Amazon UAE كيبين أكثر من 1,000 نتيجة على "flame diffuser". هذا كيخلي customer يقدر يقول: "شفتها من قبل" أو "لقيتها أرخص".

أفضل اختيار:

**Layali RainAura Lamp**

Product type:

Anti-gravity rain cloud aroma lamp / rain cloud diffuser / ambient scent lamp.

الفكرة:

جهاز ديكور عطري فيه cloud/rain visual effect، إضاءة هادئة، صوت قطرات/white noise اختياري، diffuser للزيوت، وهدية premium للغرفة، الصالون، المكتب، أو غرفة النوم.

النسخة اللي خاصنا نقلبو عليها ما تكونش cheap rain cloud عادية فقط. خاصها تكون:

- anti-gravity أو water-drop visual effect.
- تصميم premium: wood grain, stone, pearl white, matte finish, أو transparent premium dome.
- remote control.
- 500-780ml tank أو runtime محترم.
- auto shut-off.
- low noise.
- essential oil compatible.
- packaging gift-ready.
- preferably includes 2-3 premium scent oils.

## علاش هذا المنتج أحسن من flame diffuser

### Flame diffuser

Pros:

- سهل يتشرح.
- visual قوي شوية.
- رخيص في sourcing.

Cons:

- متشبع.
- متباع بأسعار رخيصة في Noon/Amazon.
- كيبان dropshipping product معروف.
- صعيب تبيعو غالي إلا إذا brand/packaging قوية بزاف.

Research signals:

- Noon UAE فيه flame diffusers من حوالي AED 48 إلى AED 160.
- Amazon UAE فيه أكثر من 1,000 نتيجة لـ "flame diffuser".
- أمثلة Noon: flame diffuser AED 48.40, AED 69, AED 72.50, AED 99, AED 120, AED 145, AED 160.

Decision:

لا نبدأ بـ flame diffuser كـ hero.

### Rain cloud / anti-gravity aroma lamp

Pros:

- visual أقوى: الزبون كيشوف cloud/rain/drop effect وكيحس أنها gadget جديدة.
- value perceived أعلى من diffuser عادي.
- مناسب للـ home decor + scent + relaxation + gift.
- فيديو UGC قوي جدا.
- ممكن يتباع كـ premium gift product.
- confirmation/delivery غالبا أحسن من beauty claims لأن الزبون عارف شنو غادي يستلم: gadget كيبان فالصور والفيديو.

Cons:

- كاين rain cloud رخيص في Noon بأسعار AED 59-139.
- خاصنا نميزو بالتصميم، bundle، packaging، warranty، وcontent.
- لازم quality testing: leakage, sound, motor noise, light quality, mist strength.

Decision:

نبدأ بـ premium version من rain/anti-gravity aroma lamp، ماشي generic cheap version.

## Why confirmation and delivery can be high

This product can have high confirmation because:

- Customer knows exactly what they are buying from video.
- It is physical/decorative, not a slow-result promise.
- It has gift value.
- It solves emotional desire fast: room ambience.
- COD customer will still want it when courier arrives if the offer looks premium.
- Price can feel justified if package includes oils and gift box.

Main risk:

If customer sees the same cheap model on Noon/Amazon, confirmation drops.

Solution:

- Source a visually better model.
- Use premium name/packaging.
- Include oils.
- Show real videos.
- Avoid generic product images.
- Avoid fake discounts.
- Use warranty/replacement trust.

## Supplier checklist

Must-have:

- Stable water flow / rain effect.
- No leaking.
- Not too loud.
- Strong visual in video.
- Auto shut-off.
- Essential oil compatible.
- UAE plug or adapter.
- Good packaging.
- Spare parts availability if filters/cotton needed.
- Can pass 24-hour test.
- Does not overheat.
- Good plastic/material feel.

Avoid:

- Very cheap lightweight plastic.
- weak LED.
- noisy pump.
- splash leaking.
- fake "anti-gravity" that looks bad in video.
- product that requires complicated setup.

Testing before ads:

1. Film it in bright room.
2. Film it at night.
3. Test with water only.
4. Test with essential oil.
5. Run 2-3 hours.
6. Check table surface for water.
7. Check noise.
8. Check if video looks premium enough for AED 299+.

## Final recommendation

Yes, aroma lamp can work, but only if we do not sell a generic flame diffuser.

Best product:

**Layali RainAura Lamp**

Best exact angle:

**Premium anti-gravity rain cloud aroma lamp with scent oils and gift packaging.**

Launch price:

Start around AED 249-299 if quality is good.

Push AED 349 only if:

- visual is very strong.
- packaging is premium.
- 2-3 oils included.
- product videos look expensive.
- device feels solid.

Do not launch until:

- samples pass leak/noise/video tests.
- the video makes people comment "فين نقدر نشريها؟"

If these conditions are met, this is a better hero product than flame diffuser because it has stronger wow factor, better giftability, and higher perceived value.
